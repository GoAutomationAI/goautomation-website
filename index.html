FCA Compliance

Your advisers are messaging clients on personal WhatsApp. That's a compliance risk that needs addressing.

For firms with multiple advisers, personal WhatsApp use is one of the most significant unmanaged risks in the business. The FCA requires firms to retain all client communications — and a personal phone is completely invisible to your compliance team. Our platform significantly reduces that risk.

⚠ What happens when a complaint arrives?

The FOS may request a full conversation history. If that conversation happened on a personal phone — and the adviser has since left or wiped their device — that evidence is gone. Your firm carries the liability.

⚠ What if the adviser leaves?

Client relationships built on personal WhatsApp walk out the door with them. Every conversation, every context, every commitment — inaccessible to your business overnight.

⚠ Can you see what's actually being said to clients?

Without a centralised system, you have zero visibility of adviser messaging. That's not just a compliance risk — it's a conduct, quality, and reputational risk too.

Post-completion client care

Consumer Duty requires ongoing client contact. Most brokers aren't doing it.

The FCA's Consumer Duty regulation, in force since July 2023, requires firms to demonstrate good client outcomes throughout the product lifecycle — not just at point of sale. Staying in touch post-completion isn't optional, it's prescribed. Most brokers are not evidencing this and are technically exposed.

We build automated WhatsApp touchpoints that contact clients at least every 6 months across their fixed rate period — whether that's 2, 5, or 10 years. Every touchpoint is logged as evidence of Consumer Duty compliance and creates natural opportunities for additional funding, finance requirements, general insurance, estate planning, and referrals.

Optional add-on: branded newsletters, birthday and holiday messages, and market updates delivered via WhatsApp, personalised to your brokerage. Keeps your firm front of mind between mortgage cycles and generates referrals.

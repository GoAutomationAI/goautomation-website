01What are cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to website owners. Cookies do not give us access to your device and we cannot use them to identify you personally unless you have provided us with personal information.

02How we use cookies

Our website (goautomation.ai) uses cookies to improve your browsing experience, understand how visitors use our site, and to support our marketing activity. We will always ask for your consent before placing non-essential cookies on your device.

03Types of cookies we use

Strictly necessary

Essential for the website to function. Cannot be disabled.

No consent required

Performance / Analytics

Help us understand how visitors use our site, such as pages visited and time spent. Data is aggregated and anonymous.

Consent required

Functional

Remember your preferences and settings to improve your experience.

Consent required

Marketing

Used to track visits and deliver relevant advertising. We do not currently run retargeting campaigns but may do so in future with your consent.

Consent required

04Third-party cookies

  • Google Analytics — website usage statistics
  • Google Tag Manager — managing tracking scripts
  • Meta Pixel — marketing measurement (if enabled)
  • GoHighLevel — embedded forms or chat widgets
  • Video platforms such as YouTube or Vimeo (if embedded content is present)

These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies. We recommend reviewing them directly if you have concerns.

05Managing and withdrawing consent

When you first visit our website, you will be shown a cookie banner allowing you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time by adjusting your browser settings or clicking the cookie settings link in our website footer.

06How to control cookies in your browser

  • Google Chrome — support.google.com/chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox — support.mozilla.org
  • Safari — support.apple.com
  • Microsoft Edge — support.microsoft.com

07Retention of cookie data

Session cookies are deleted when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain for a set period — typically between 30 days and 2 years — unless deleted earlier.

08Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation or our services. The current version and effective date are shown at the top of this document.
